## View relationship diagram

While you are configuring relationships between tables, you can review the overall relationship diagram from the **Data configuration** page.

**Steps**

On the **Data configuration** page, click the **Relationship diagram** button.

<Image
    src="/docs/docimages/generators/set-table-relationships/view-relationship-diagram-01-click-button.webp"
    alt="MOSTLY AI - Generator - Click the Relationship diagram button"
    width={900}
    height={300}
/>

**Result**

The relationship diagram appears in a modal window. The relationship diagram shows all foreign keys between tables and indicates which are the context relationships.

# Delete an organization

When you delete an organization, all resources and data associated with the organization are deleted.

<Callout type="warning">To avoid a loss of resources, [transfer them to a user or another organization](/organizations/manage-resources#transfer-an-resource-to-a-user-or-organization) before deleting the organization.</Callout>

**Prerequisites**

Only a team member with the Admin role can delete an organization. For more information, see [_Roles and permissions_](/organizations/roles-and-permissions).

**Steps**

1. On the organization page, click **Settings**.
   <Image src="/docimages/organizations/delete-01-click-settings.webp" alt="Delete an organization - 01 - Click settings" width={700} height={300} />
2. Scroll to the bottom.
3. Click **Delete organization**.
   <Image src="/docimages/organizations/delete-02-click-delete.webp" alt="Delete an organization - 02 - Click Delete" width={700} height={300} />
4. In the confirmation dialog, click **Yes, delete organization**.

**Result**

All resources and files associated with the organization are deleted.

Deleted resources include:

- generators
- synthetic datasets
- connectors

The association of existing team members with the organization is removed. No other changes are applied to the team members' accounts.

# Report a misrepresented organization

At MOSTLY AI, we want to deliver the best possible experience to our users and ensure that the information provided on our Platform is accurate and organization profiles are represented by the correct entities. If you believe that an organization is misrepresented on our Platform, we encourage you to report it to us.

If you find an organization profile that you believe is misrepresented, send us an email at `support@mostly.ai` with the link to the organization profile explaining why you believe it is misrepresented. 

We will review your report and take the necessary actions to ensure the integrity of our Platform.

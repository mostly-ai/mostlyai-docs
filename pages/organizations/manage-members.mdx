# Manage organization members

## Invite members to an organization

**Steps**

1. On the organization page, click **Settings**.
    <Image 
        src="/docs/docimages/organizations/delete-01-click-settings.webp"
        alt="Delete an organization - 01 - Click settings"
        width={700}
        height={300}
    />
2. Under **Members**, click **Copy** to copy the invite link.
    <Image 
        src="/docs/docimages/organizations/invite-02-under-members-click-copy.webp"
        alt="Invite members to an organization - 02 - Copy invite link"
        width={700}
        height={300}
    />
3. Share the invite link with the person you want to invite.

When the members accepts, they are then listed as part of the members.

<Image 
    src="/docs/docimages/organizations/accept-01-organization-members.webp"
    alt="Accept an organization invitation - 01 - Organization members"
    width={700}
    height={300}
/>

## Accept an invitation

1. Open the invitation link.
2. In the dialog, click **Accept**.
    <Image
        src="/docs/docimages/organizations/accept-01-click-accept.webp"
        alt="Accept an organization invitation - 01 - Accept"
        width={700}
        height={300}
    />

**Result**

You are now part of the organization members.

## Remove a member

**Steps**

1. On the organization page, click **Settings**.
    <Image 
        src="/docs/docimages/organizations/delete-01-click-settings.webp"
        alt="Delete an organization - 01 - Click settings"
        width={700}
        height={300}
    />
2. Under **Members**, click **Remove** for the team member.
    <Image 
        src="/docs/docimages/organizations/remove-02-under-members-click-remove.webp"
        alt="Remove a member from an organization - 02 - Click Remove"
        width={700}
        height={300}
    />
3. In the confirmation dialog, click **Yes, remove member**.

**Result**

The team member no longer has access to the organization and its assets.
